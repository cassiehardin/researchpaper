Benefits of Modern Technology

By: Cassie Hardin

Improved communication

Modern technology has improved communication across the world. One way technology has advanced Communication is through electronic communication devices. Technology has made communication more convenient by creating more ways to communicate. There are different sources and devices invented that has made it easily accessible for all people. Some can include the brands such as Apple, Galaxy, or Android. Whatever device you chose to use, it makes communication easy because email, social media, text, FaceTime, or just by calling someone makes communication from person to person better. The advancements of modern technology can also make it easier to communicate with a large number of people at one time. This is beneficial for a lot of people that need to get a quick or long message out to many people at one time. For example, many businesses and academic institutions rely on mass emailing to send information to a large number of people. This is why modern technology has better the communication across the world.

Improves knowlegde

Modern technology has improved knowledge by allowing students and adults to access information quickly. If we are not sure of something then the internet is easily accessible and we are able to go and find the answer. Technology also allows us to take online college classes in order to get our work done. So if you are in a place where it is hard for you to attend school then classes online are available for your use. This improves your knowledge and allows you to get a job. Another example can be coding class! With our computers we are able to learn how to construct a website. This is something I never thought I would do. But with the knowledge I have learned so far by the tutorials and online information I am able to create my own page by coding. Books are also online so you can read more books and increase your knowledge but reading online books.

Better Learning Techniques

Technology has changed educational materials. The quality is improved. Now books are colorful, contains images, diagrams, charts. Now they are inspiring. Even it’s easy to understand the topic of the chapter from its image. It’s helping to improve the image of the topic in students mind. The advanced modern technology has also enhanced research. The technology allows students to research information faster and gives them another resource to use for papers or classwork. The information is expanded and gives them better learning opportunities. Instead of having to look through a bunch of books they have the answer at their fingertips. Since the introduction of computers, tablets, and mobile phones, there has been a great scope of education applications that allow learners to learn various concepts in a playful manner. When learning becomes fun with the modern technology students become more engaged in what they are learning and allows them to learn at their own pace.

Saves Time

The advancements of modern technology has saved a lot of time. The use of phones, computers, and other technology has saved time for all people. The use of technology with cars have saved the trouble of us having to walk to places. Another example of a new advancement can include the use of Siri. This element has saved people time by allowing us to speak into the phone and asking for the answer instead of us actually having to go to the search engine and finding it yourself. You might say to yourself we have become very lazy considering the fact that we don’t have to lift our finger very far to find information. But besides that the advancements and easy access to information has saved a lot of time. We take advantage of the easy access because we don’t realize how much time it would take to write a whole paper only using books.

Expanded creativity

Social media has become a really popular thing that many people use to show their creativity. Social media is also encouraging creativity among our current generation of students. Through social media programs such as Tumblr, Pinterest, and Instagram, students are combining technology with inspiration, to create a wealth of resources and information available globally. Many people have found their career with modern technology. Their careers have benefited them by giving them something they really love to do. It has opened the mind of people and with technology it allows people to access other people’s ideas. In STEM subjects, the use of technology and the need for creativity go hand-in-hand. Technology provides the opportunities for learners of STEM students to be more creative than ever before. Students have the opportunity to build virtual models of their devices, programs, robots, and other gadgets. Modern technology is still advancing and makes it a competitive world for people to invent and create more for us to use.
